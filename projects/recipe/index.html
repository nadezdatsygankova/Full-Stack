Nadia's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup milk

For the filling:

  • 1 1/2 cups white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease and flour a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix together the warm water, flour, baking soda, and salt. Stir in the milk until smooth.
  3. Using a rubber spatula, stir the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until combined.
  4. Spread the dough evenly into the prepared pan.
  5. Bake for 25 minutes, or until a cake tester inserted into the center comes out clean.
  6. While the cake is baking, combine the sugar, brown sugar, butter, eggs, and vanilla in a separate bowl until smooth.
  7. Spread the filling evenly over the baked cake.
  8. Bake for an additional 10 minutes, or until the filling is set.
